# Complete 7 Week Golf Workout Programme

| Exercise | Prescription |
|----------|-------------|
| Dead hangs | 30 seconds |
| Band pull-aparts | 20 reps |
| Face pulls | 15 reps |
| Scapular push ups | 10 reps |
| Thoracic rotations | 10 each side |

### Golf Speed Training
- 10 driver swings at 100% speed
- 30 sec rest between swings
- Focus: speed not accuracy

## Sunday — Rest

## 7 Week Progression

| Weeks | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Weeks 1–2 | Learn technique / moderate weight |
| Weeks 3–4 | Increase weight 5–10% |
| Weeks 5–6 | Push heavier / near failure last set |
| Week 7 | Deload to 80% weight / mobility focus |
